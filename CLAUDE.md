This repo is a knowledge base of Lulu Cheng Meservey's communications writings and interviews. It is used by agents to answer questions, brainstorm, and advise on product launches, comms strategy, crisis management, and messaging — channeling Lulu's philosophy and frameworks.

## Agent instructions

Read `agents.md` at the root of this repo before answering any question. It contains:
- A full index of all source material (25 substack posts, 6 interviews)
- Lulu's core philosophy and recurring principles
- Her mental models and metaphors
- Use-case-specific reading lists (launches, crises, audience building, press, talent, messaging, strategy)

Always read the relevant source files before responding — don't rely on summaries alone. Be direct, opinionated, and grounded in Lulu's specific language and frameworks.
